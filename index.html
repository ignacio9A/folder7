The Magic of Filipino Music

To understand the magic of Filipino music, you first need to embrace it. Embracing it means being proud of our music and accepting what it means to be Filipino.

Harana

The serenade is usually performed with a guitar, and it involves singing a heartfelt song in front of the girl and her family. It's both a romantic and emotional gesture.

Emotional Resonance in Filipino Music

Many Filipino songs are deeply emotional, often reflecting intense feelings and personal experiences. This is especially true for songs with high notes, where the emotion is palpable and resonates with listeners.
